About PaulPhiloPress

Your Trusted Partner in Professional Laundry Care

Our Story

PaulPhiloPress was founded in 2026 with a simple mission: to make professional laundry services accessible and convenient for everyone. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted service provider serving thousands of happy customers.

We believe that everyone deserves clean, fresh clothes without the hassle of doing laundry themselves. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to providing exceptional service with unmatched quality and reliability.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide the highest quality laundry and dry cleaning services while making the process as convenient as possible for our customers. We aim to become your go-to laundry service partner by offering:

  • Professional and expert cleaning services for all fabric types
  • Fast and reliable pickup and delivery
  • Competitive pricing without compromising quality
  • Exceptional customer service every single time
  • Eco-friendly cleaning methods
