Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What are your service hours?
We operate Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. You can schedule pickups and deliveries during these hours through our online platform.
❓ How long does cleaning take?
Standard cleaning takes 24-48 hours from pickup. Express services are available for an additional fee if you need faster turnaround.
❓ What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Opay, POS, Cash), and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely on our platform.
❓ Is there a service charge for pickup and delivery?
No! We offer free pickup and delivery for orders over ₦20,000. For smaller orders, a minimal delivery fee may apply.
❓ How can I track my order?
Once you create an account and place an order, you can track it in real-time through your account dashboard. You'll also receive SMS and email updates at each stage.
❓ What if I'm not satisfied with the service?
Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with your service, contact us within 48 hours of delivery, and we'll make it right or provide a full refund.
